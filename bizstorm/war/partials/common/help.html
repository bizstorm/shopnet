Frequently Asked Questions

How do i search for a specific offer ?

You may type in the search keyword (for eg : "iphone 5") in the first search text box and type in your location in the second search box and click the "SCAN" button to get the results pertainign to your search. You may modify the keywords if you are not satisfied with the results.

I have some feedback/query. How should i contact you folks ?

You can fill in the "Contact Us" form at www.scantheshops.com/contact-us or directly write to us at hi@scantheshops.com. Alternatively, you may use the Feedback widget which appears on the middle right corner of each page.

How can my business, brand or agency work with ScanTheShops?

Whether you're a normal retailer/shopkeeper, a national chain, or a brand, ScanTheShops can provide you with tools to engage with your customers and help increase your revenues and profits. If you are interested in partnering with us, you may send a mail to welcome@scantheshops.com with the details of your business and our team shall get in touch with you.
