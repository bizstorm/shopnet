What is ScanTheShops?

ScanTheShops is a free app that helps you find great deals on products, services and restaurants in your nearby locations.
Whether you're setting off on a shopping trip or trying to pick out the best deal at your local restaurants or looking for best discounts on services like beauty treatment, plumbing and more, ScanTheShops is the perfect companion.

Who we are?

We are a team of young engineers lead by IITians who want to provide accurate information related to local shopping. We are committed to provide the most relevant offers from local merchants based on your shopping needs. All our offerings are driven by technology and we truly believe tecchnology can solve complex day-to-day problems.

Why are we offering free services?

Our passion is to solve problems through technology which can change the day-to-day life of users and touch their hearts. All our offerings are free of cost to our users because we firmly believe that right to correct information is a must and that right should not come with a cost.
